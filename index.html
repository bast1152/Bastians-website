MasterMedia – A Media Experience Reinvented

MasterMedia is a dream in the making—a fully integrated media center designed for both terminal enthusiasts and everyday users alike. The goal is to create a seamless platform that functions on HTPCs, Android TV, and Linux devices, combining both GUI and terminal-based interfaces, all remote-controllable.

Think Kodi, but with a elegant, responsive, and beautiful twist. MasterMedia aims to deliver not just functionality but a refined experience that blends power, style, and flexibility.

it's still just fantasy, but big things often start with a spark of imagination.
